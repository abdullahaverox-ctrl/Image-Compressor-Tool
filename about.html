About Us

A free, private, browser-based image toolkit.

Welcome to Image Tools — a small, focused website built to make everyday image tasks effortless. We provide free online tools that let you compress, resize, and convert images directly in your browser. There is nothing to download, no account to create, and no waiting in queues.

Our Mission

We believe that simple, useful tools should be available to everyone without paywalls or privacy trade-offs. Big media files slow down websites, fill up phone storage, and make sharing harder than it needs to be. Our goal is to give you fast, reliable image utilities that fix those problems in seconds.

What Makes Us Different

  • Free forever. Every feature is available to every visitor with no hidden costs, ads inside the tools, or signup walls.
  • Privacy first. Images are processed entirely on your device using the HTML5 Canvas API. Files never leave your browser, are never uploaded to a server, and are never stored.
  • Lightweight and fast. The site is built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript so it loads quickly even on slow connections.
  • Mobile friendly. Use Image Tools on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop — without losing any features.

Tools We Offer

  • Image Compressor — shrink JPG, PNG, and WebP files with a quality slider.
  • Image Resizer — resize images to any width and height with optional aspect-ratio lock.
  • Image Converter — convert between popular formats (coming soon).
