Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Image Tools ("we", "our", or "the website") handles information when you visit our site. We have built this site with privacy as a core principle. The short version: your images stay on your device and we collect as little information as possible.

1. Image Processing

All image compression, resizing, and conversion happens entirely inside your web browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. When you upload an image to one of our tools:

  • The file is read locally by your browser.
  • It is processed in your browser's memory.
  • The result is saved directly to your device when you click download.

No file is uploaded to our servers. We have no ability to view, copy, store, or share the images you process. As soon as you close the browser tab, your image is gone from this site.

2. Personal Information

We do not ask you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use our tools. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifying information from visitors using the image tools. If you choose to contact us through the Contact page, any details you provide there are used only to respond to your message.

3. Cookies

The website itself does not set tracking cookies. Your browser may store small amounts of data (such as remembering your last selected tool tab) using local browser storage; this information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

If we add advertising or analytics in the future, those third-party providers may set cookies on your device. We will update this Privacy Policy and provide more information at that time.

4. Third-Party Services

We load a small set of web fonts from a public font provider so the site looks consistent across devices. Loading these resources may expose your IP address and basic browser information to that provider, as is standard for any website that uses external fonts. We do not share any other information with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences. Because we do not collect personal information from any visitor, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page occasionally to stay informed.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.
